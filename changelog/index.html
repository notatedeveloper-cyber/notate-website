June 12, 2026 Public Beta

Notate is live on the Chrome Web Store

After months of internal testing, Notate is now available to everyone as a free public beta. Install it from the Chrome Web Store, hit the hotkey, and start capturing UI feedback in seconds.

  • One-keystroke overlay — open the annotation panel without leaving the page you're reviewing.
  • Pin any element — click any DOM element to attach a comment directly to it.
  • Screenshots included — a screenshot of the current viewport is captured automatically with each annotation.
  • Push to Linear or Jira — connect your workspace once and send annotations straight to your issue tracker.
  • Local-first — all pins, comments, and screenshots live in your browser. Nothing is sent to our servers unless you export it.

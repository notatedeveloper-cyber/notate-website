What slows teams down on Marker.io.

Capable, but heavy. Here's what users run into — and what it costs the people relying on the feedback.

1

The essentials are locked behind $149/mo

Console logs, session replay, and two-way sync only arrive on the Team plan. Starter is widely described as "almost a demo," and there's no free tier — just a 15-day trial.

Impact

Small teams either overpay or file bugs with half the context developers need to reproduce them.

2

Clients need a separate portal login

Guest reviewers collaborate through a dedicated portal that requires its own account and password.

Impact

Clients forget logins and drop off, feedback stalls, and your team ends up babysitting two sets of accounts.

3

Every report is a multi-step form

Open the widget, capture, annotate, then set type, priority, and assignee before anything posts to your tracker.

Impact

Logging an issue takes minutes, so reviewers report fewer of them — and real bugs quietly slip through.

4

Per-website limits cap you early

The Starter plan restricts you to a handful of websites (5), with more gated behind higher tiers.

Impact

Agencies and teams juggling many client sites hit the ceiling fast and get pushed into an upgrade they didn't plan for.

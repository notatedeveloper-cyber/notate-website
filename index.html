How it works

Point. Comment. Log.

Click any UI element, leave feedback and create a ticket instantly – without leaving the browser.

Step 01

Pick what's broken

Click any UI element on the page — or drag a region for a screenshot.

Step 02

Add feedback

Step 03

Log instantly ⚡️

Press Shift+Enter to log as a Linear or Jira ticket.

FAQ

Questions, answered honestly.

Is it really free?
Yes. The full extension is free during the open beta — no seats, no usage caps, no card required. If we ever charge, beta users get a long runway and a fair price. We won't pull the rug.
How do you make money?
Today, we don't — there's a tip jar for people who want to chip in. Post-beta, we'll likely sell a team plan with shared workspaces and SSO. Individual use will stay generous.
Does it work with my tracker?
Native one-click export to Linear and Jira on day one. GitHub Issues, Asana, and Notion are next on the list. You can also copy a clean Markdown block and paste into anything.
Is my data private?
Pins stay local until you push them. Screenshots are taken in the browser and only leave your machine when you send a ticket. We don't read your pages, and we don't track what you look at.
Can I use it on internal tools and localhost?
Yes. Notate runs on any page your browser can open — staging, admin dashboards, localhost, VPN'd internal apps. Nothing leaves the extension until you choose to send a ticket.

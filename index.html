Questions,
answered honestly.

Is it really free?+
Yes. The whole extension, free. No seats, no usage caps, no card. If we ever charge for a team plan later, individual use stays generous and beta users get a long runway.
Where does my data go?+
Straight from your browser into Linear or Jira, over your own auth. We don't read your pages. We don't store pins in our cloud. We don't keep a copy in your browser either.
Does it work on internal tools and localhost?+
Yes. Notate runs on any page Chrome can open — staging, admin dashboards, localhost, VPN'd internal apps.
What gets attached to the ticket?+
A screenshot, the URL, the viewport, the user agent, the selector path of the element you pinned, and any console errors that happened on the page. Structured so a downstream agent can pick it up without further prompting.
How do I get my team on it?+
Send them the Chrome Web Store link. There's nothing to provision, no SSO to configure, no seats to buy. Each person connects their own Linear or Jira and they're in.

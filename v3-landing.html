Frequently asked questions

What exactly does Notate capture? +

Notate captures a full-page screenshot, the DOM path and CSS selector of the pinned element, the complete URL, browser name and version, OS, viewport dimensions, and any console errors present at capture time. All of it attaches to the ticket automatically.

Does it work on any website? +

Yes. Notate works on any URL you have open in Chrome, including staging environments, localhost, and production sites. The only exception is browser-built-in pages like chrome://settings.

Which trackers does it support? +

Linear and Jira are both fully supported. Connect your workspace, choose a project, set a priority, and push — all from the Notate panel. GitHub Issues, Notion, and ClickUp are on the roadmap.

Is my data private? +

Screenshots and context data are sent only to the tracker you choose. Notate does not store your data on any external server. Everything goes directly from your browser to your ticket tracker.

Is Notate really free? +

Completely free during the open beta. A paid team tier with unlimited usage and advanced features is planned for later. Early beta users will receive a meaningful discount when pricing launches.
